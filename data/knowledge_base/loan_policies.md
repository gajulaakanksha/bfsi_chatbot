# Loan Policies and Guidelines

## Home Loan

### Eligibility

- Age: 21 to 65 years at loan maturity
- Minimum income: Rs. 25,000/month (salaried) or Rs. 3 lakh/year net profit (self-employed)
- Credit score: 700+ preferred; 650+ considered with higher interest rate
- Employment stability: Minimum 2 years in current job (salaried) or 3 years in business (self-employed)
- Existing EMI obligations should not exceed 50% of net monthly income

### Loan Amount and LTV

- Maximum Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio:
  - Up to Rs. 30 lakh: 90% of property value
  - Rs. 30 lakh to Rs. 75 lakh: 80% of property value
  - Above Rs. 75 lakh: 75% of property value
- Minimum loan amount: Rs. 5 lakh
- Maximum loan amount: Based on eligibility and property value

### Interest Rates

- Floating rate: Repo Rate + spread (currently 8.25% to 9.50% p.a.)
- Fixed rate: Available at 0.25-0.50% premium over floating rate
- Rate reset: Every 3 months for repo-linked loans
- Women borrowers receive 0.05% concession

### Tenure

- Minimum: 5 years
- Maximum: 30 years (subject to borrower not exceeding 70 years at maturity)
- Longer tenure reduces EMI but increases total interest outgo

### Documents Required

1. Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
2. Address proof (utility bill, rental agreement)
3. Income proof:
   - Salaried: Salary slips (3 months), Form 16, bank statements (6 months)
   - Self-employed: ITR (3 years), business financials, GST returns
4. Property documents: Sale agreement, title deed, approved plan, NOCs
5. Passport-size photographs

### Processing Fee

- 0.25% to 0.50% of loan amount (negotiable for high-value loans)
- Legal and technical verification charges: Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 15,000

## Personal Loan

### Eligibility

- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Minimum income: Rs. 20,000/month
- Credit score: 750+ for best rates
- Employment: Minimum 1 year in current organization (salaried)
- Existing customers get preferential treatment

### Loan Details

- Amount: Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 25 lakh (up to Rs. 40 lakh for premium customers)
- Interest rate: 10.50% to 16% p.a. (varies by profile)
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Processing fee: 1% to 2.5% of loan amount
- No collateral required
- Disbursement: Within 24-72 hours for pre-approved customers

### Prepayment

- Floating rate: No prepayment charges (RBI mandate)
- Fixed rate: Up to 2% foreclosure charges after 12 months
- Partial prepayment: Allowed after 6 EMIs (minimum Rs. 10,000)

## Education Loan

### Eligibility

- Indian national with confirmed admission to a recognized institution
- Course: Graduation, post-graduation, professional courses, or vocational training
- Co-borrower: Parent or guardian mandatory

### Loan Details

- Up to Rs. 7.5 lakh: No collateral required
- Rs. 7.5 lakh to Rs. 50 lakh: Collateral required (property, FD, etc.)
- Interest rate: 8.50% to 11.50% p.a.
- Moratorium: Course duration + 12 months or 6 months after getting a job (whichever is earlier)
- Repayment tenure: Up to 15 years after moratorium

### Tax Benefits

- Section 80E: Full interest deduction for 8 years from start of repayment
- No limit on the deduction amount
- Available only for loans from recognized financial institutions

## Vehicle Loan

### Car Loan

- New car: Up to 90% of ex-showroom price; interest 7.50% to 9.50%
- Used car: Up to 80% of valuation (vehicle age < 5 years); interest 10% to 14%
- Tenure: 1 to 7 years
- Processing fee: 0.5% to 1%

### Two-Wheeler Loan

- Up to 85% of on-road price
- Interest: 10% to 16% p.a.
- Tenure: 1 to 5 years
- Quick processing: Same-day disbursement possible

## Gold Loan

- Loan up to 75% of gold's market value (as per LTV norms)
- Interest rate: 7% to 14% p.a.
- Tenure: 3 months to 3 years (renewable)
- Processing: 30 minutes to a few hours
- Minimum gold purity: 18 carat
- Repayment options: Bullet payment, regular EMI, or interest-only with bullet principal

## Loan Against Fixed Deposit

- Up to 90% of FD value
- Interest: FD rate + 1% to 2%
- No separate documentation (FD as collateral)
- Cannot exceed FD maturity date
- Available as overdraft or term loan

## General Loan Policies

### NPA Classification

- Standard: Regular payments
- Sub-standard: Overdue for 90+ days
- Doubtful: NPA for 12+ months
- Loss: Identified as uncollectable

### Recovery Process

1. Reminder calls and notices after 30 days overdue
2. Formal demand notice after 60 days
3. SARFAESI notice after 90 days (for secured loans)
4. Legal proceedings if unresolved
5. Settlement or write-off as last resort

### Restructuring Guidelines

- Available for genuine financial distress
- Options: Tenure extension, EMI reduction, moratorium
- Requires written application with supporting documents
- May impact credit score
- Maximum one restructuring per loan lifecycle
