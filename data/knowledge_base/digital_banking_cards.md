# Digital Banking, Cards, and Payment Systems

## Digital Banking Channels

### Internet Banking

- Available 24x7 for account management, fund transfers, bill payments
- Registration: Use debit card details + registered mobile number, or visit branch
- Security: Two-factor authentication (password + OTP)
- Features: Account statements, NEFT/RTGS/IMPS, bill payments, FD booking, loan applications
- Session timeout: 5-10 minutes of inactivity
- Transaction limits: Customizable through settings

### Mobile Banking

- Secure app-based banking on smartphones
- Features: All internet banking features + UPI, QR scanning, cardless ATM withdrawal
- Security: Device binding, biometric authentication, MPIN, app lock
- Best practices:
  - Download only from official app stores
  - Never use on rooted/jailbroken devices
  - Avoid public WiFi for banking transactions
  - Keep app and OS updated
  - Enable transaction alerts

### UPI (Unified Payments Interface)

- Real-time bank-to-bank transfers using UPI ID or QR code
- Transaction limit: Rs. 1 lakh per transaction (Rs. 2 lakh for some categories)
- Daily limit: Usually Rs. 1 lakh (varies by bank)
- Available 24x7
- No charges for person-to-person transfers
- UPI ID format: yourname@bankname
- Multiple bank accounts can be linked
- UPI Lite: For small transactions (up to Rs. 500) without PIN

### BHIM App

- Government-backed UPI app
- Supports all bank accounts via UPI
- Features: Send/receive money, check balance, transaction history
- Works on all smartphones with internet connectivity

## Payment Systems

### NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)

- Batch processing: Every 30 minutes
- Available 24x7 (including holidays)
- No minimum or maximum amount limit
- Free for online transactions (savings accounts)
- Branch-initiated: Charges Rs. 2 to Rs. 25 based on amount
- Settlement time: Within 2 hours

### RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement)

- Real-time, individual transaction processing
- Minimum: Rs. 2 lakh (no upper limit)
- Available 24x7 for online transactions
- Branch timings: 9 AM to 4:30 PM on working days
- Free for online savings account transactions
- Fastest mode for high-value transfers

### IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)

- Instant interbank transfers 24x7
- Maximum: Rs. 5 lakh per transaction
- Charges: Rs. 5 to Rs. 15 (varies by bank and amount)
- Requires beneficiary IFSC + account number, or MMID + mobile number
- Fastest option for values under Rs. 2 lakh

### NACH (National Automated Clearing House)

- For recurring payments: EMIs, insurance premiums, SIPs, utility bills
- Mandate-based auto-debit system
- Setup: Submit mandate form at branch or e-mandate through net banking
- Can be modified or cancelled before next debit date
- Bounce charges apply if insufficient balance

### Cheque Truncation System (CTS)

- Electronic cheque clearing without physical cheque movement
- Local clearing: T+1 (next business day)
- CTS-2010 standard for all cheques
- Cheque validity: 3 months from date of issue
- Crossed cheques can only be deposited, not encashed
- Account payee cheques credited only to named payee

## Debit Cards

### Types

1. Classic/Silver: Basic features, lower limits
2. Gold: Higher limits, some rewards
3. Platinum: Premium features, lounge access, higher limits
4. RuPay: Domestic usage, government-backed, lower merchant charges

### Features and Limits

- ATM withdrawal: Rs. 25,000 to Rs. 1,00,000/day (based on card type)
- POS transactions: Rs. 1 lakh to Rs. 5 lakh/day
- Online transactions: Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 2 lakh/day
- International usage: Must be enabled separately
- Contactless payments: Up to Rs. 5,000 without PIN

### Security Features

- EMV chip technology
- PIN-based authentication
- OTP for online transactions
- Magnetic stripe for backward compatibility
- Ability to set custom limits via app
- Instant block/unblock through mobile app
- International transaction toggle

### Card Replacement

- Lost/stolen: Block immediately, replacement in 5-7 working days
- Damaged: Visit branch for replacement
- Charges: Rs. 200-500 for replacement
- Express delivery available at additional cost

## Credit Cards

### Types

1. Entry Level: No annual fee, basic rewards
2. Cashback: 1-5% cashback on purchases
3. Rewards: Points per spend, redeemable for gifts
4. Travel: Air miles, lounge access, travel insurance
5. Fuel: Fuel surcharge waiver, accelerated rewards on fuel
6. Premium: High annual fee, concierge service, unlimited lounge access

### Key Features

- Interest-free period: 18-50 days (if full payment made by due date)
- Minimum due: 5% of outstanding or Rs. 200 (whichever is higher)
- Late payment fee: Rs. 500-1,300 based on outstanding
- Interest on revolving credit: 2.5-3.5% per month (30-42% p.a.)
- Cash advance fee: 2.5-3% of amount (minimum Rs. 300-500)
- Foreign currency markup: 2-3.5% on international transactions

### Credit Card Best Practices

1. Always pay full outstanding amount by due date
2. Keep credit utilization below 30% of limit
3. Never share card details, CVV, or OTP
4. Review statements regularly for unauthorized charges
5. Set up auto-pay for at least minimum due
6. Notify bank before international travel

### EMI Conversion

- Convert large purchases into EMIs at 12-18% p.a.
- Tenure: 3, 6, 9, 12, or 24 months
- Processing fee: 1-2% may apply
- Available for purchases above Rs. 2,500-5,000
- Pre-approved EMI offers on e-commerce platforms

## Digital Wallet and Prepaid Cards

- Closed wallet: Can be used only with the issuer (store-specific)
- Semi-closed wallet: Can be used at multiple merchants (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Open wallet: Can be used for purchases and cash withdrawal
- KYC requirements for higher wallet limits
- Maximum wallet balance: Rs. 2 lakh (full KYC)

## Forex Cards

- Multi-currency prepaid card for international travel
- Better exchange rates than cash conversion
- Load currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, SGD, AED, etc.
- Reload while traveling through net banking
- Chip and PIN security
- Customer care for emergency card replacement abroad
- Insurance coverage included in premium forex cards
